About Us

Freda Anyanwu

CEO Kaycee's Events

Kaycee’s Events Is a Luxury full service event management company that is specialized to cater for both individual and corporate clients. Since 2017, our top priority is to create unforgettable experiences by going the extra mile to satisfy client’s expectations. Kaycee’s Events is founded by Freda Anyanwu out of her passion for creative designs, detailing and organisation. Adept to innovation, Kaycee’s Events has successfully managed multiple projects which has earned us an African award nominee recognition from Maya Awards Africa in 2019. Our main goal is deliver the Ultimate Client Experience with a team of well trained professionals to deliver from start to finish.
